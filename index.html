About the Exhibition:

We have partnered with 3 Irish artists to provide Ireland's first NFT art exhibition. We brought this collaboration to life to connect a new, vibrant, and growing community around digital art here in Ireland. We realise the potential in this space and are keen to unlock our artist's potential by providing a platform to share their work.

Get your one-of-a-kind artwork:
On display at the exhibition will be printed copies of the artworks, the artists will be present to share their inspiration and thinking behind their work.

The artworks will also be made available for online purchase following our exhibition. However, for your best chance to be apart of the event, make your way down to the gallery. Upon purchase, you will receive the NFT and a high-quality printed copy of the work to take home.

Our Mission

Here at Galway Art Musuem, we aim to give Irish artists a platform to showcase their work at one of Irelands's most prestigious galleries. We hold weekly unique exhibitions which highlight emerging trends in the art community.

As well as our exhibtions, we have a collection of historical art pieces, we aim to showcase Irish art, both old and new. Galway Art Gallery is located in the heart of Galway, so if you have further queries about our mission, feel free to drop in for a chat!
