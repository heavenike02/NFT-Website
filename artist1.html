Artist:

Finbar Mchugh

An Irish artist and meditator Finbar McHugh orignates from our hometown, a fellow galweigan.

He thinks that art can make a space feel different and enable us to become present, think, and let go.

About the Exhibition:

Finbar uses art as a method of meditation, and this is evident in all of his work. His studio work is a vibrant, emotional, and very personal experience that comes to life and changes in natural light, opening up new vistas of comprehension. Finbar's studio and residence in Woodquay provides a venue for readings, meditation classes, and other artistic events. In a welcoming, soothing, and vibrant environment, he invites people to think on and explore their own path to wellbeing.

Whether you want to examine the components that are illuminated subtly or take in the visual in its wholesome This serene environment gives countless opportunities for inner clarity and progress, so take some time to sit with Finbar for advice. When he's not in the studio, Finbar delights in the small pleasures of life, such as going for walks with his dog George, interacting with the neighborhood, and being in nature.

We collaborated with Finbar to bring Irish Art a step closer to the future. By creating unique NFTS of his beautiful artworks on the blockchain. After the exhibition, All artwork will be auctioned off,the winners will obtain the related NFT as proof of ownership and authenticity.
